# Code Of Conduct

CoinFox follows the Contributor Covenant v2.1 spirit: open participation,
respectful disagreement, and clear maintainer enforcement.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in CoinFox and Eastern Pine Intelligence spaces
welcoming and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Disagree about ideas, code, tests, and evidence, not people.
- Explain risk clearly when reviewing market, model, or safety changes.
- Respect different experience levels.
- Help keep the project educational and transparent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or doxxing.
- Spam, credential harvesting, or malicious automation.
- Sharing secrets, private logs, private keys, seed phrases, or account data.
- Misrepresenting CoinFox output as certain financial advice.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer safety guidance.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, request edits, restrict
participation, or ban contributors who violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to maintainers. If the report involves a
security issue, leaked credential, or abuse technique, follow
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Maintainers should respond as quickly as reasonably possible and protect reporter
privacy where they can.
