# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/)
v2.1 in spirit. The short version:

- **Be kind.** Disagree about code, not people.
- **Assume good intent.** Most folks are trying to help.
- **No harassment, no discrimination, no doxxing, no spam, no shilling coins.**
- **This is a BTC project**, but altcoin discussions are fine if they help BTC
  context. Promotion of unrelated tokens is not.
- **Maintainers may remove content or contributors** that violate these norms.

To report an issue, open a private security advisory on GitHub, or email the
maintainer listed in the repo. We'll respond as soon as we reasonably can.
